
At the moment, iotkit-comm supports only communication plugins. The following communication plugins are supported:

a) mqtt <BR>
b) zmqpubsub <BR>
c) zmqreqrep <BR>
d) iotkit (for communicating with Intel's enableiot cloud) <BR>
